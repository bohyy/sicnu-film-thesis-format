# 川师 2026 版论文格式规则摘编

Source: `(2026版)四川师范大学博士硕士学位论文撰写打印要求及格式范本.zip`, including the school template and GB/T 7713.1-2025. This file is a working condensation for formatting tasks, not a replacement for the source archive.

## 1. General Requirements

- 学位论文内容应完整、准确，立论正确，推理严谨，文字简练，层次分明，数据真实可靠。
- 主要使用中文撰写，采用《通用规范汉字表》规范汉字。
- 标点符合 GB/T 15834；数字用法符合 GB/T 15835；日期和时间符合 GB/T 7408.1。
- 术语、符号、代号全文统一。首次出现的专业术语、缩略词应加以说明；外文术语或缩略词首次出现时，在中文译名后用圆括号注明原词全称。
- 计量单位采用国家法定计量单位，符合 GB 3100、GB/T 3101、GB/T 3102。

## 2. Thesis Order

川师模板要求论文通常按以下顺序组织：

1. 封面
2. 论文独创性及使用授权声明
3. 中文摘要
4. 英文摘要
5. 目录/目次页
6. 引言或绪论
7. 正文
8. 结论
9. 参考文献
10. 附录
11. 致谢
12. 在校期间的科研成果

If a part is absent in the user's thesis, do not fabricate content unless asked.

## 3. Page Setup

- Paper: A4, 210 mm × 297 mm.
- Printing: double-sided is expected; printed cover/back cover use light yellow textured paper.
- Margins:
  - Top: 30 mm
  - Bottom: 25 mm
  - Left: 25 mm
  - Right: 25 mm
  - Gutter: 10 mm
  - Header distance: 23 mm
  - Footer distance: 18 mm
- Body and back matter page numbering: Arabic numerals.
- Front matter page numbering: Roman numerals, separate from body numbering.
- Header/footer text: 小五号宋体.
- Header from abstract page to final page:
  - Left-side page header centered: `四川师范大学博（硕）士学位论文`
  - Right-side page header centered: current chapter title/一级标题名称
  - Header rule may be a single line or upper-thick/lower-thin line.
- When modifying a document, verify that section breaks do not accidentally reset or remove the required headers.

## 4. Fonts and Spacing

- Body: 小四号宋体.
- Body line spacing: fixed 20 pt.
- Body paragraph: first-line indent 2 Chinese characters; continuation lines align to left edge.
- English and Roman characters: Times New Roman, upright unless italics are required by convention.
- Notes: 小五号宋体.
- References: 五号宋体.

## 5. Heading Hierarchy

The source file uses Arabic heading numbering, but this skill enforces the user's required Chinese hierarchy:

- `第一章 标题` — 三号黑体; chapter starts on a new page; chapter title occupies 2 lines.
- `第一节 标题` — 四号黑体.
- `一、标题` — 小四号黑体.
- `（一）标题` — 小四号楷体.
- `1. 标题` — use only when the thesis needs a fifth level; avoid excessive levels.

Spacing:

- Headings and immediately following lower-level headings do not need blank lines between them.
- Between a previous正文 paragraph and a following heading, leave one blank line or equivalent paragraph spacing.
- Each chapter should begin on a new page.
- Keep heading numbering and title text flush left unless a college-specific template says otherwise.

## 6. Abstract

Chinese abstract:

- Abstract title: 小二号宋体.
- `专业`、`研究生`、`指导教师`: 小四号黑体.
- Student name and supervisor name: 小四号楷体.
- Abstract body: 小四号宋体.
- `关键词`: 小四号黑体.
- Keywords: 3-8 terms. They should reflect the thesis topic and appear as meaningful terms in the thesis.
- The school template says master's abstract should be within 1000 Chinese characters; GB/T 7713.1-2025 says Chinese abstracts are generally 300-1000 characters unless special needs require more.
- Avoid formulas, charts, uncommon symbols, and reference citations in abstracts.

English abstract:

- Starts on a new page after Chinese abstract.
- Content corresponds to Chinese abstract.
- English/Roman characters use Times New Roman unless the template field specifies otherwise.
- English keywords correspond to Chinese keywords.

## 7. Figures

- Figures include diagrams, charts, maps, photos, screenshots, flowcharts, frame captures, interface images, etc.
- Figures should be self-explanatory and appear close to the relevant text.
- Figure numbering follows source convention: `图2.1`, `图2.2`, etc. Use Arabic chapter number for figure/table numbering even though chapter headings use `第二章`.
- Figure caption: below the figure, centered.
- Caption format: `图2.1 图题`.
- Figure caption font: 五号黑体.
- Figure legend/explanation: 小五号宋体.
- Photos and screenshots should be clear, with adequate contrast. Add scale markers only when the image requires measurement.

## 8. Tables

- Tables should be self-explanatory and appear close to the relevant text.
- Numbering: `表2.1`, `表2.2`, etc.
- Table title: above the table, centered.
- Caption format: `表2.1 表题`.
- Table caption font: 五号黑体.
- Table body text: 小五号宋体 unless readability requires slight adjustment.
- Table notes: 六号宋体.
- Prefer academic three-line tables.
- If a table continues to another page, repeat the table number and optionally title with `(续)` above the continuation, and repeat the table header.

## 9. Formulas

- Display formulas on separate lines, indented or centered with enough spacing from body text.
- Formula numbers use Arabic numerals in parentheses, aligned at the right. If many formulas exist, number by chapter, e.g. `(2.1)`.
- Long formulas may break after relation symbols or operation signs.

## 10. Cross-References

- Check textual references after editing headings, figures, tables, formulas, footnotes, or references.
- Figure references such as `如图2.1所示` must match an existing figure caption.
- Table references such as `见表2.1` must match an existing table title.
- Chapter/section references should follow the Chinese heading hierarchy, e.g. `第一章`、`第一节`, not `1.1` unless the user specifically uses Arabic chapter references in body text.
- Footnote markers in正文 must match same-page bottom footnotes.
- Bibliographic footnotes and final `参考文献` entries should remain mutually consistent when both are present.
- Do not leave placeholder references such as `图?.?`, `表XX`, `见上图`, or stale numbers after inserting/deleting content.

## 11. Notes and Footnotes

- The source template distinguishes explanatory notes from bibliographic references, but this user's thesis convention uses bottom-of-page circled footnotes primarily for reference/source notes. Preserve that convention unless the user asks to convert all citations to bracketed in-text references.
- Keep notes limited; do not overuse.
- Use bottom-of-page footnotes under a separator line, matching the sample layout.
- Footnote text: 小五号宋体.
- Required local style: use circled-number superscript markers such as `①②③`, not plain Arabic digits and not image markers.
- Footnote numbering restarts on every page: each page begins again with `①`.
- Each正文 circled marker should have a matching same-page bottom footnote. If the footnote cites a source, keep its bibliographic elements consistent with GB/T 7714 and with the文末参考文献 list.

## 12. References and Reading Lists

- `参考文献` is the collection of sources explicitly cited in the thesis.
- Place references after the正文/结论 on a new page.
- All cited sources must appear in the reference list.
- Sources read but not cited should not be mixed into `参考文献`; if necessary, place them in an appendix titled `参考书目`.
- Follow GB/T 7714-2015. See `reference-format.md`.

## 13. Appendix and Ending

- Appendices are optional.
- Appendix number and appendix title each occupy one centered line above the appendix content.
- Each appendix usually starts on a new page; several short appendices may be continuous.
- Appendix material may include detailed data, methods, interview outlines, images, rare documents, transcripts, large tables, video/audio/data-set descriptions, etc.
- `致谢` may be a separate page and should thank only organizations or individuals with substantial help.
- `在校期间的科研成果` uses reference-like formatting for publications and achievements.
